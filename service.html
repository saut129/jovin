Welcome to CBE Library Services

At CBE, we understand the unique needs of international students, and we are committed to providing a supportive and enriching environment for your academic journey.

1. Accommodation Services

The College has a limited number of rooms in its hostels. To secure accommodation at the College Hostels:

  • Complete registration and request accommodation online (through CoSIS)
  • After system approval, report to the Dean of Students' office
  • Receive and pay the accommodation invoice through bank

Priority in room allocation is given to our international students on a first-come, first-served basis.

2. Library Services

The mission of the College of Business Education library is to enrich the learning, teaching, and research journey of our college community in today's digital world.

We offer:

  • A physical library with extensive collections (Open Mon-Fri 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM, Sat 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM)
  • Access to e-resources through various databases
  • Expert assistance from our librarians

Visit: https://cbe.ac.tz/library
Email: hlib@cbe.ac.tz

3. Transportation Options

Navigating the city of Dar es Salaam is made easy with various transportation options:

  • Public buses (daladalas) and rapid transit buses (mwendokasi)
  • Taxis including Uber, Bolt, Taxify, and licensed taxis
  • Ferries for crossing the harbor
