CBE Online Systems

COSIS CBE is an online system for managing student records, enabling students and staff to access key academic services such as course registration, exam results, and fee payments.

The CBE online application portal simplifies the application process for prospective students applying to certificate, diploma, bachelor's, postgraduate diploma, and PhD programs. The portal allows applicants to apply to any of the four CBE campuses and manage their applications efficiently from anywhere.

Applicants can log in or register via the portal. For assistance, applicants can contact the respective campuses via the details below.
